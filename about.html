Our Story

Born from a Love of Ancient Art

Henra was founded in 2012 by master mehndi artist Layla Nair, who grew up watching her grandmother apply henna at every family gathering. What started as a small studio in Chennai quickly grew into one of the region's most trusted names in henna artistry.

Today, our team of certified artists serves hundreds of brides, families, and event planners every year. We believe henna is more than decoration — it is a language of celebration, identity, and love.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Every design we create is guided by a deep commitment to artistry, integrity, and community.

Our Mission

To preserve the timeless art of mehndi by delivering exceptional, handcrafted henna experiences that honor cultural roots while embracing modern creativity.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted henna brand in South Asia, recognized for the purity of our materials, the mastery of our artists, and the warmth of our service.

Our Values

Authenticity, craftsmanship, cultural respect, and customer delight are the pillars on which Henra stands. We never compromise on quality.
