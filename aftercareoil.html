Application Guide

How To Use Aftercare Oils

Follow these four simple steps for the best post-treatment results.

1

Cleanse Gently

Use a mild, fragrance-free cleanser. Pat dry — never rub.

2

Warm the Oil

Dispense 3–5 drops and gently warm between fingers.

3

Press & Massage

Press onto skin using upward motions. Do not drag.

4

Let It Absorb

Allow 5–10 minutes. For best results, apply before bed.

Questions & Answers

Aftercare Oil FAQs

Everything you need to know before adding our oils to your routine.

When should I apply the oil after a treatment?
Apply the oil within 30 minutes of completing your treatment while the skin is still slightly warm and pores are open. This ensures maximum absorption of the active botanical compounds.
Can I use these oils on sensitive skin?
Yes. All Henra aftercare oils are dermatologist-tested, free from synthetic fragrances and parabens, and formulated specifically for post-treatment sensitive skin.
Are Henra oils non-comedogenic?
Absolutely. Every oil in our range has a comedogenic rating of 2 or below, making them safe for all skin types including acne-prone and oily skin.
