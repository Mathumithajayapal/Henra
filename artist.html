Common Questions

Henna Artist FAQs

Everything you need to know before booking your session.

How long does a henna session take?
Session length varies by design complexity. A simple hand design takes 20–30 minutes. Bridal full-hand designs typically take 2–4 hours.
Is henna safe for all skin types?
Henra exclusively uses natural, organic henna paste free from black henna chemicals. We recommend a patch test 24 hours before your session.
How long does henna last?
Natural henna typically lasts 1–3 weeks depending on skin type, placement, and aftercare. Hands and feet tend to stain darkest and longest.
Do artists travel to my location?
Yes! All Henra artists offer both studio appointments and home/venue visits. A travel fee may apply depending on your location.
